Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my favorite proverbs?

Tap the heart icon on any proverb to add it to your favorites.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred language from 24 available options.

How do I take quizzes?

Navigate to the Quiz tab. Choose your preferred level and quiz type, then start learning!

How do I remove ads?

You can remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option in Settings.

Is my progress saved?

Yes! All your learning progress is automatically saved on your device.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All 300 proverbs are available offline. Only ads require internet.

Troubleshooting

  • App crashes: Try restarting the app or your device
  • Data not saving: Ensure the app has storage permissions
  • Ads not loading: Check your internet connection
  • Purchase not restored: Use "Restore Purchases" in Settings
