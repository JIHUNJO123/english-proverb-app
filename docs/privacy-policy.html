Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how English Proverb 300 collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

English Proverb 300 is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Learning Progress: Stored locally on your device only
  • Favorites: Saved locally on your device
  • App Settings: Language preferences, theme settings (stored locally)
  • Anonymous Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve the app

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers for ad personalization
  • General location data (country/region level)

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

3. In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed through Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

4. Data Storage

All your learning data, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal learning data to external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For advertisements
  • MyMemory Translation API: For translations (no personal data sent)
  • Google Play / App Store: For in-app purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Since data is stored locally, you have full control over your data.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all app data through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
